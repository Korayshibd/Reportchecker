How to use this tool:

  • Paste your original email list in the "My Email List" textarea (one email per line)
  • Paste the reported/bad email list in the "Reported / Bad Email List" textarea (one email per line)
  • Click "Check Match" to find emails that exist in both lists
  • Results will show matched emails (red) and clean emails (green)
  • Use "Download as CSV" to save matched or clean emails
  • Use "Clear All" to reset the tool
